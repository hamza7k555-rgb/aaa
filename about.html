Our Company We set up new standards in the hosting industry,
by offering our services for free! 
Wispbyte was founded in early 2023 with the goal of providing both game and general application hosting. Over time, we realized that the demand for our services was overwhelming, and we chose to focus on what we did best: reliable and accessible application hosting. Since making that shift, we've seen thousands of users explore and grow comfortable with cloud hosting—many for the first time—thanks to the opportunities our platform has provided.
Wispbyte's mission 
Our mission is to empower users by making cloud hosting more accessible and intuitive. We believe cloud technology is a key part of the future, and we're committed to educating and supporting our community as they grow in this space. Making a meaningful impact through knowledge and accessibility remains at the core of everything we do.

About Us

From a simple idea to a growing platform, here's how Wispbyte evolved over time.

April 2023

The Beginning

PylexNodes was aquired by the current CEO & changed the whole project's perspective by mainly focusing on generic application hosting.

July 2023

High Demand

We've noticed higher demand for our services, so we invested a lot of our time to improve our software & the overall user experience.

December 2023

Community Growth

More users started supporting our project by promoting it to friends, spreading it heavily by word of mouth.

January 2024

Owned infrastructure

We decided that it would be a good idea to start self hosting a part of our infrastructure, in order to lower down costs and scale up further.

June 2024

Milestone achieved

We've reached 50,000 users, and we're still growing!

December 2024

Milestone achieved

We've surpassed 100,000 total servers ever created on our free infrastructure!

January 2025

More revenue

We've surpassed 500 active customers paying for their premium server on a recurring basics.

March 2025

Rebrand

What once was called PylexNodes now became Wispbyte with a more ambitious initiative!

Today

Looking Forward

Continuing to innovate and expand our services while maintaining our commitment to free, reliable hosting.
